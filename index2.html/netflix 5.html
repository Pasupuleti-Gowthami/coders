BACK BENCHERS SEASON -1

This web series intends to take you back to the classroom of the 90's. In the coming episodes we will bring to you those fond memories that most of us can relate to and have experienced in some form. These light hearted videos will not only take you down memory lane, but will also make you burst into laughter. Some of the episodes will make you think through and retrospect. The majority of the story telling is around the two characters "Manya and Ganya", the back benchers and will revolve around the various situation that they find themselves in and how they deal with these tricky, weird situations.
